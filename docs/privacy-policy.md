# Privacy Policy — Goods Match 3D

_Last updated: 2026-06-14_

Goods Match 3D ("the Game") is designed to respect your privacy. Host this page at a
public URL and use that link in the Google Play "Privacy policy" field.

## What we collect
**Nothing personal.** The Game does **not** collect, transmit, or sell any personal
information. There are no accounts and no sign-in.

## Data stored on your device
Your game progress (level, coins, stars, settings, statistics) is saved **locally on
your device** using browser/app local storage. It never leaves your device and is not
sent to any server. Deleting the app or clearing its data removes it permanently.

## Third parties
This build does not integrate real third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. If a
future version adds ads or analytics, this policy will be updated and the Google Play
"Data safety" form will be revised accordingly before release.

## Children
The Game is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any
data from anyone, including children.
